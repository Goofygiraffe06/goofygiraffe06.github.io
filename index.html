I'm 18, studying Computer Science Engineering at Anurag University, Hyderabad. I work on cryptographic systems and zero-trust architectures. Currently developing a novel authentication protocol that eliminates all server-side secrets, and implementing cryptography on onboard computers for our student satellite.

I fly A320 simulators in FlightGear and solve Project Euler problems in my free time. I'm (soon) a member of the Cryptology Research Society of India (CSRI).

I believe the future belongs to systems that don't require trust, where cryptographic proofs replace blind faith, and security emerges from mathematical certainty rather than institutional promises. My work aims to build computing infrastructure that remains secure even when everything else fails.
